How to Build Good Credit

Having good credit and good credit history is important to your adult life. It can impact wheather you qualify for a mortage, car loan and even down to the interest rates you'll be paying on the loans. By practicing good credit tatics early, you can increase your chances to obtain a higher credit score rating. Here are three practices you can take to help jump start your path to good credit building:

  • Open a bank account in your name.
  • Apply for commerce credit cards.
  • Pay monthly bills on time and wait for credit to report.
